factions:
  - name: "..."

Each item must have:
- name (string) — **English** fantasy faction name, even when other fields follow the **session language policy** (system message); prefer specific, institutional, or regional labels over generic ominous compounds unless the campaignConcept already centers that tone

- resources (1 sentence)
- currentDisposition (1 sentence about attitude toward outsiders, travelers, or the local power balance — not toward a named player character)

A bare YAML sequence of those mappings at the root is also acceptable.
